Delivery Policy

Last updated: July 9, 2026

BunnyEra LLC primarily provides digital services. Deliverables are sent electronically by email, shared document, file link, or other agreed online channel.

1. Delivery method

Service deliverables may include research documents, written content, website copy, templates, workflow documentation, or other agreed digital materials. We do not require physical shipping for standard digital services.

2. Delivery timeline

Delivery timelines depend on the agreed service scope and customer response time. A project timeline is confirmed before work begins whenever possible.

3. Customer review

Customers should review delivered materials promptly and request revisions within the agreed review period. Delays in providing information or feedback may extend the timeline.

4. Physical products

If BunnyEra LLC later sells physical products or merchandise, shipping terms, carrier information, costs, and estimated delivery times will be provided at checkout or in the applicable order confirmation.

5. Contact

For delivery questions, contact support@bunnyerapro.com.
