\# BunnyEra Public Website Docs



This folder stores public website notes and page planning for the BunnyEra showcase site.



\## Purpose



The public website introduces BunnyEra to users, developers, partners, and community members.



It should explain:



\- What BunnyEra is

\- What the ecosystem includes

\- What products exist

\- How AI Company works

\- Where to find documentation

\- How to join the community



\## Website Sections



Current homepage sections:



\- Hero

\- Ecosystem

\- Products

\- AI Company

\- Community



\## Content Rules



Public website content should be:



\- Clear

\- Professional

\- Public-safe

\- Easy to understand

\- Brand-consistent

\- Free of secrets



\## What Not To Include



Do not publish:



\- API keys

\- Private tutorials

\- Customer data

\- Internal credentials

\- Payment information

\- Identity documents

\- Private business documents

\- Unapproved private screenshots
