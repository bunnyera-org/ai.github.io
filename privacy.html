Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 9, 2026

BunnyEra LLC provides AI-assisted ecommerce operations and digital business services. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when customers or business partners contact us or use our services.

1. Information we collect

We may collect business contact information, project requirements, company details, website links, communication history, and documents voluntarily provided by customers for service delivery or verification.

2. How we use information

We use information to respond to inquiries, provide services, prepare digital deliverables, communicate with customers, maintain business records, prevent misuse, and comply with applicable legal or platform requirements.

3. Payment and financial information

We do not intentionally store full payment card numbers or sensitive payment authentication details on this website. Payments, if applicable, are processed through third-party payment providers or bank transfer channels.

4. Sharing information

We do not sell customer information. We may share limited information with service providers, professional advisors, compliance partners, or authorities when required to operate our business, deliver services, or comply with law.

5. Data security

We use reasonable administrative and technical measures to protect business information. No online transmission or storage system is fully secure, so customers should avoid sending unnecessary sensitive information.

6. Data retention

We retain business records only as long as reasonably necessary for service delivery, accounting, compliance, dispute resolution, and legitimate business purposes.

7. Contact

For privacy questions, contact BunnyEra LLC at support@bunnyerapro.com.
